Catch the Stick

₹30 (per person)

• Players: Five players stand in a circle, each holding a stick. • Objective: Catch the stick of the player on your right before it hits the ground. • Setup: Each player stands in a circle with some distance apart, holding a stick vertically in front of them. One player signals the start of the game. • Gameplay: At the signal, all players let go of their sticks simultaneously. Each player must quickly move to their right and try to catch the stick of the player next to them before it falls to the ground. • Scoring: Each successful catch earns a point. If a player misses the stick and it hits the ground, no points are awarded. • Rounds: The game is played in multiple rounds. After each round, players return to their original positions and repeat the process.
